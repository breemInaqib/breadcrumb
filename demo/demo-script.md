# Breadcrumb demo script

1. Open Patchwork. Point out its description, current goal, and the recent turning points rather than tasks or status metrics.
2. Open **History** and scan the sequence from reusable templates, through the experiment and discovery, to the section composer pilot.
3. Select **Add breadcrumb**.
4. Record a meaningful new moment, for example:
   - Type: `Discovery`
   - Title: `Pilot teams review flows together before publishing`
   - What happened: `Three pilot teams invited a teammate to review the inline preview before publishing.`
   - Why: `The preview made the onboarding flow concrete enough to discuss without opening the product.`
   - Outcome: `Collaborative review emerged as a valuable behavior to validate next.`
5. Save it and show that it appears in the chronological history.
6. Open **Story**. Show that the project’s current-state section now incorporates the new outcome.
7. Select the citation for the new breadcrumb and show that Breadcrumb returns to the exact source moment in History.
8. Reload the page to demonstrate that the new project memory persists locally.
